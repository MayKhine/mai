Welcome

Therapy that meets you where you are.

I'm Mai Tun, PhD, a licensed psychologist with 17 years in practice. I offer a calm, compassionate space to slow down, stay grounded, and reconnect with what matters most.

My work focuses on anxiety, depression, relationship issues, life transitions, caregiving, prenatal and postpartum seasons, and support for healthcare professionals.
