My Story

Therapy built on trust, not just technique.

I'm Mai Tun, PhD, a licensed psychologist in Portland, Oregon with 17 years in practice. My work centers on adults and elders navigating anxiety, depression, relationship concerns, caregiving, postpartum changes, grief, and life transitions.

I aim to offer therapy that feels grounded, respectful, and collaborative. Together, we slow things down enough to understand what is happening and identify practical steps toward change.

I also offer support for healthcare professionals and can provide therapy in Burmese.

My Approach

Evidence-based care with a human heart.

Every person who walks through my door is unique. That's why I draw from multiple therapeutic traditions rather than applying a one-size-fits-all approach.

  • Anxiety & depression — building steadier coping and self-understanding
  • Relationships — exploring patterns, boundaries, and communication
  • Life transitions — support through change, loss, caregiving, and new roles
  • Pregnancy & postpartum — care during emotionally complex family transitions
  • Healthcare professionals — space for the stress and responsibility of care work
